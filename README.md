Cosmic ResearchPilot AI Platform - Complete Documentation

## **General Description**
Cosmic ResearchPilot is a revolutionary quantum-cognitive AI research platform that transforms how researchers interact with scientific literature. Unlike conventional AI tools, it presents research as a multidimensional exploration across parallel knowledge dimensions, using quantum physics metaphors to create an immersive, intuitive research experience. The platform combines semantic search, vector embeddings, workspace management, and context-aware AI assistance within a visually stunning cosmic interface that makes complex research feel like exploring the universe.
